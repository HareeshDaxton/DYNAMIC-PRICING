PROJECT NAME: "Multi-Channel Dynamic Pricing Intelligence System"
 
BUSINESS PROBLEM (The Real Deal):
---------------------------------
E-commerce sellers on platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify struggle with:
• Competitors changing prices 5-10 times daily
• Lost sales due to being overpriced by ₹10-20
• Inventory sitting because they're priced too high
• Manual price checking across 3-5 platforms is impossible
• No idea what the "optimal" price is at any moment
 
This costs them 15-30% in potential revenue.
 
YOUR SOLUTION:
--------------
An AI-powered system that:
1. Scrapes competitor prices across multiple channels in real-time
2. Analyzes historical sales data to understand price elasticity
3. Predicts optimal price using ML (considering: demand, inventory, competitor 
   prices, time of day, seasonality)
4. Suggests dynamic price adjustments to maximize revenue
5. Provides a dashboard showing: current price vs optimal, revenue impact, 
   competitor trends
 
TECH STACK:
-----------
• Web Scraping: Selenium/Scrapy (for competitor data)
• Data Storage: SQLite/PostgreSQL
• ML Models: 
  - XGBoost for price elasticity prediction
  - Time series forecasting (Prophet/LSTM) for demand
  - Reinforcement Learning (Q-learning) for optimal pricing
• Backend: FastAPI
• Frontend Dashboard: Streamlit with Plotly charts
• Deployment: Docker + Cloud (Render/Railway)

✓ Revenue impact calculator ("You could earn ₹45,000 more this month")
